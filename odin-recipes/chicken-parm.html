Chicken Parmesan

Home

Description

My sample of a Chicken Parmesan recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Bread crumbs
  • spaghetti noodles
  • 3 eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Cover the chicken with salt and pepper
  2. Scramble the eggs
  3. Sprinkle four onto both sides of the chicken
  4. dip chicken into the scrambled eggs
  5. cover chicken with bread crumbs
